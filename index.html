Written in pure JavaScript, the datepicker comes without any other dependencies. Yes, you cannot believe it but it is true, you don't need jQuery!

Bower support

If you want to be up to date with our latest releases, just install the datepicker with Bower, we support it.

Customizable themes

The datepicker comes with easy to customize themes which makes it easy to fit for your needs.

Easy to Contribute

The datepicker is build with Grunt and LESS and this makes it easy to contribute.
